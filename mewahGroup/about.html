About US


We are established in 1980’s in the Federal Territory of Labuan with 3 outlets and had expanded to Kota Kinabalu with 5 outlets, Sandakan with 2 outlets, Lahad Datu with 1 outlet and Tawau with 3 outlets. In the 90’s we had move our Head Office to Kota Kinabalu in line with the group expansion.


In the early days KAINMEWAH started off as one of the Bumiputera textile supplier to the small retailers around Sabah and has initiated a great change in local entrepreneurship.


KAINMEWAH directions of “One Branch One District” resulted in with KAINMEWAH now has 16 Branches all over the Sabah. KAINMEWAH is now planning to expand their branches in the nearby town in sabah. In order to fulfill the needs of Malaysian public, kainmewah is importing new fashionable textile goods from the foreign countries and exposing to the Malaysian public. KAINMEWAH is the chief promoter of the haline with our mission to assist the local traders & entrepreneur by work hand in hand with them, not only that we have built a reputable brand but we have also create job opportunity for the local people in various department.


And to add to the team, we also employed staff from other countries such as Indonesia and the Philippines to work hand in hand with the local people.
